About Us

About Travela Travela was founded in 2021 with a clear mission: to transform the way people experience travel and accommodations in Bangladesh. Our goal is to make finding and booking places to stay simple, secure, and enjoyable—while also supporting local communities and businesses.

Whether you’re planning a quick weekend getaway or a long-term stay, Travela connects you with quality, affordable, and convenient lodging options across the country.

Our Story Travela is proudly developed by Expert Travel & Tourism Limited, a private limited company based in Dhaka, Bangladesh, and officially incorporated in 2020. We launched our mobile app in June 2021, available on both iOS and Android, to make travel planning easier than ever before.

Behind the Vision Meet Mir Ishtiaque, a passionate filmmaker and experienced Airbnb host who witnessed first-hand the gaps in Bangladesh’s short-term rental market. Motivated by these challenges, he set out to build a platform that would redefine local travel—creating a solution specifically tailored to the needs of travelers and hosts in Bangladesh.

Why Travela? Travela is designed for today’s traveler. From cozy rooms and modern apartments to luxury resorts and private villas, we offer a wide variety of stay options to suit every style and budget. With a user-friendly interface and a focus on trust and convenience, Travela is making travel smoother, safer, and more enjoyable for everyone.
